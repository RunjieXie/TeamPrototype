Welcome to the FAU Hoodie Design Contest

Everyone can create and submit his or her own designs for new FAU hoodies.
FAU members can also participate in the selection of the winning design.
The winner will receive a hoodie with his or her own design as a prize.



Your design must fulfill following requirements

Your design must contain only 4 colors
Your design is intended for the front of the hoodie
Your design should be uploaded in the following file formats: GIF, PNG, JPEG, vector graphic
Your should use the new FAU logo, not the old one
The logo must appear at least once in the lower right belly area
